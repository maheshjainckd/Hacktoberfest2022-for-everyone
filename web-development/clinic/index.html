HEART DISEASE IN NEPAL

From Nepal's perspective, the outbreak of heart disease has been on the rise due to the socio-economic changes, industrialization, urbanization, development of civilization and changes in the diet and lifestyle of the people of the last two decades

Since most heart diseases are related to lifestyle, lifestyle changes can also prevent these diseases. Therefore, I heartily request all concerned to take the first step towards making their lifestyle healthy during heart disease treatment.
